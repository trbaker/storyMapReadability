ArcGIS Storymap Flesch-Kincaid Readability

One of the most common ways teachers evaluate the utility of instructional content is with the Flesch-Kincaid grade level readability score. This score (e.g. 4.9) tells an educator at approximately what grade and month, a typical student should be able to read and understand content.
